Nagasri went on over the next 6 months to own and operate several successful Norman Cosmetics stores in and around the Hyderabad, many of which she increased the sales more that 100% in the short time that she owned them. But she had a bigger dream! A dream of owning one of the top salon and spas in the Hyderabad.
